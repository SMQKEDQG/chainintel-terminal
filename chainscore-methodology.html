ChainScore Methodology

A transparent, multi-dimensional digital asset rating framework

Overview

ChainScore is ChainIntel's proprietary composite rating for digital assets, scored 0–100. It evaluates every asset across six weighted dimensions using real-time and historical on-chain, fundamental, and market data.

Unlike opaque institutional ratings, ChainIntel publishes its full methodology. Bloomberg hides methodology. We publish ours.

Every sub-dimension score is visible in the terminal. Every data source is attributed. Every methodology revision is logged in the changelog.

The 6 Dimensions

Each dimension is scored 0–100 independently, then combined using the weights below.

On-Chain 20%
Fundamentals 20%
Regulatory 15%
Smart Money 20%
Technicals 15%
Adoption 10%
1 On-Chain Health
20%

Metrics

Active addresses (30-day trend), transaction volume, network value-to-transactions ratio (NVT), hash rate or validator count.

Scoring

90+ >90th percentile of 12-month range
50 50th percentile (median baseline)
<20 <10th percentile — network activity decline

Sources

Glassnode, CryptoQuant, Etherscan

2 Protocol Fundamentals
20%

Metrics

Protocol revenue (30-day), TVL if applicable, developer commits (GitHub), fee generation.

Scoring

80+ Revenue growth >20% MoM
50 Flat revenue — stable but no growth signal
<30 Declining revenue and developer activity

Sources

Token Terminal, DefiLlama, GitHub API

3 Regulatory Exposure
15%

Metrics

SEC classification status, ETF approval status, MiCA compliance, FATF travel rule status.

Scoring

90+ Classified commodity + ETF approved
50 Pending classification or partial compliance
<20 Active enforcement action

Sources

SEC EDGAR, EU MiCA registry, ChainIntel regulatory database

4 Smart Money Position
20%

Metrics

Whale wallet accumulation/distribution (30-day), exchange reserve changes, Nansen-labeled institutional flows.

Scoring

85+ Net accumulation >2x distribution
50 Neutral — balanced accumulation and distribution
<30 Net distribution — smart money exiting

Sources

Whale Alert, Glassnode, CryptoQuant

5 Technicals Risk
15%

Metrics

MVRV Z-Score, funding rates, open interest ratio, 200-day moving average position.

Scoring

80+ MVRV <1.5 + positive funding + above 200d MA
50 Mixed signals across indicators
<30 Overextended — high MVRV, negative funding

Sources

CoinGlass, CoinGecko, Glassnode

6 Adoption Metrics
10%

Metrics

Unique address growth (90-day), DEX volume share, institutional custody availability, exchange listing count.

Scoring

85+ >15% address growth + top-10 DEX volume
50 Stable adoption — no significant growth or decline
<30 Declining unique addresses and volume share

Sources

CoinGecko, DefiLlama, IntoTheBlock

Composite Calculation

ChainScore = (OnChain × 0.20) + (Fundamentals × 0.20) + (Regulatory × 0.15)
+ (SmartMoney × 0.20) + (Technicals × 0.15) + (Adoption × 0.10)

Each sub-dimension score is calculated independently using the metrics and thresholds defined above, then combined using fixed weights. The composite score is updated in real time as new data arrives.
