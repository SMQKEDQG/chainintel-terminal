ISO 20022 Asset Intelligence · Institutional Adoption Tracker
Bloomberg Cannot Do This
ISO 20022 is a global financial messaging standard being adopted by SWIFT, central banks, and major payment networks worldwide. Think of it as a universal language for money — it allows banks, payment systems, and blockchains to communicate seamlessly. Cryptocurrencies built on ISO 20022 (like XRP, XLM, HBAR, ALGO, XDC, IOTA, and QNT) are positioned to integrate directly with the global banking system, potentially handling trillions in daily payment volume. This makes them fundamentally different from speculative tokens — they are infrastructure plays.
